About Us



Hey to whoever's reading this. Our group is called CryptoWare. We're a new and active development group motivated by our love for programming. We are mainly located in the United States however, we do have assets located across the globe. Our group is currently developing several products related to discord, however we aim to create products on well-known platforms like Roblox, Unity and eventually Steam. Our main goal is to become one of the biggest development groups to ever exist.

We do have a community for communication and feedback purposes. This community can be found on Discord. Some things to know about our Discord server is that it has 300+ members, there are giveaways, updates regarding all of our products (individual details are included) and a lot more. If your worried about it not being safe, then don't be. We've taken a numerous amount of precautions to ensure the safety of everyone in there. The environment is safe for everyone 13+. You can support our group by simply joining our Discord server or by donating to our group. If you would like to contribute to our group's cause by donating then just let us know via email at support@cryptoware.info.
